How to dispose of bulky waste

Once you have booked the collection online, the booking team will aim to call you back within the next working day for chargeable requests. They will then advise you of the next available collection slot and your collection will be made within three days. Adding or removing items after booking is not possible. If you are booking a bulky waste collection with a concession request (for example if you are receiving Housing Benefit or Council Tax Support), once you have booked the collection online our booking team will aim to call you back within two working days. The team will then advise you of the next available collection slot and your collection will be made within three days. Adding or removing items after booking is not possible. Please let us know your availability for a call-back during office hours, 9am to 5pm.
